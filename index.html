About Me

Greetings! I am Kgaogelo Lekganyane, a Software Engineer with roots deeply entrenched in the arts of film, photography, and dramatic narratives. My academic odyssey began with a spirited year at Wits, delving into dramatic arts, before steering towards the technical mastery of Python at HyperionDev's Software Engineering bootcamp. My professional voyage set sail in 2006, charting a course through the multifaceted realms of media. From the editing suite to the director's chair, I've orchestrated a symphony of projects that not only embellished my craft but also painted my world with the broad strokes of creative fulfillment. These experiences have forged my path through storytelling, encapsulating the essence of narrative in every frame and line of code. The transition into software engineering was a seamless integration of my artistic sensibilities with my passion for technology, allowing me to sculpt digital experiences that resonate with users on a profound level.
